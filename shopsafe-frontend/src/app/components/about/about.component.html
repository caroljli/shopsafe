Philosophy

ShopSafe is a tool designed to help ease the decision fatigue that comes with deciding what is the safest place to go shopping during COVID-19. The purpose of this tool is to provide a crowdsourced method of evaluating the safety of a grocery store to give users a more informed shopping experience.

Usage

This tool is meant to be used in concurrence with good judgement and awareness of local regulations. The ShopSafe Score is calculated using the check-in and county statistics based on user input and the New York Times COVID-19 dataset. This is not a deterministic metric but rather a decision-making tool.
